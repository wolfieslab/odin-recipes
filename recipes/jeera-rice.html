Jeera Rice

Description

A fragrant and mildly spiced rice dish flavored with cumin seeds. A great side dish for dals and curries.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1¾ cups water
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse basmati rice 2–3 times and soak for 15–20 minutes. Drain.
  2. Heat ghee/oil in a pot or pressure cooker.
  3. Add cumin seeds (and bay leaf if using), let them splutter.
  4. Add soaked and drained rice, sauté gently for 1–2 mins.
  5. Add water and salt.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and cook 10–12 mins (or pressure cook for 1 whistle).
  7. Let it rest 5 mins before fluffing with a fork.
  8. Serve hot with curries or dal.
